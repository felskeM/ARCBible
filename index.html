1. Intro to NetSuite

  1. Open NetSuite from your preferred browser and log-in with the given credentials.
  2. You're given the OFFICE role and WAREHOUSE MANAGER (WMS) role in NetSuite. In the beginning, you will mosly be using the WMS role as it's mostly for creating orders, marking them as picked/packed/shipped, and inventory items. The OFFICE role can't mark orders as picked/packed/shipped however, it can view and create invoices/bills for customers. The OFFICE role has a lot of capabilities to edit subsidiaries and customers that the WMS doesn't have that will be useful in the future.
  3. Familiarize yourself and personalize the dashboard layout and menu options as you see fit.

2. Entering RFI (Flooring Inc.) Orders

Begin by opening the Outlook inbox to the Customer Service account (customerservice@athleticrc.com) and double clicking the PO PDF so that it's open in Adobe.

  1. Right-click on the PO attachment and save to the "RFI Purchase Orders" folder.
  2. Right-click again on the PO attachment and print the attachent to any non-label printer. You can now delete the email.
  3. Mark-up the order for the warehouse and send the file to parsing (arcorderparsin@pliteq.com)
  4. When marking-up, put on the top right: Shipping state, material and thickness, shipping due date, and the N.S. S-number.

4. Entering Other Customer Orders

  1. Search for the customer's name in the inbox.
  2. Save the PO to the correct folder.
  3. Print and mark up as usual with shipping info and N.S. S-number.
