1. Intro to NetSuite in Pliteq - ARC

  • Login & Roles: Open NetSuite in your browser and log in using company credentials. You will be using the Office and Warehouse Manager roles.
  • Customize: Feel free to personalize your dashboard layout.
  • When creating sales orders or having our AI create the orders for us, you will have to ensure that the PO we are given matches what's on NetSuite--price, quantity, item, etc.

2. Entering RFI (Flooring Inc.) Orders

  1. From your inbox, save the POs to the RFI Purchase Orders folder. Then print the POs.
  2. Mark-up the printed document and send the file to parsing (arcorderparsin@pliteq.com). The contents of the email should say "Ship {ESD} from {WI or AZ}" and nothing else as we use the DocParser AI to create SOs in NetSuite from the POs.
    • If using GMail, you'll have to edit the subject to "Emailing: Purchase Order_{PO#}"
    • If you have setup an IMAP configuration from Gmail to Outlook, just go to file explorer and right-click to send file as an attachment.
  3. Mark-up info: In the top right of the doucment, write in order (from top to bottom): thickness and color first, ESD below, finally the sales order number last.
    • The ESD can be determined as follows: Next-Day = next business day, Quick Ship = next 3 business days, no comment = next 5 business days.

  4. RFI gives us their preferred shipping method--Usually being FedEx (if within dimension limits), R&L, SAIA, or ABF.
    • For FedEx orders, you will need to login to our FedEx account and create a new shipment.
    • For all carriers, we have a BOL template that you can overwrite and create 2 hard copies of. You will also need to put the address and SO# on our skid tag template and print out one. The warehouse will let you know if they ever need more skid tags.

3. Entering AFM (American Floor Mats) Orders

  1. Open and extract the PO: Print the PO and view the PDF in Adobe and go to the edit tab. Extract the PDF as separate files to the AFM Orders folder.
  2. Send: Email the individually extracted files to arcorderparsing@pliteq.com with the same subject and contents as the RFI format.
    • DocParser cannot create an SO from AFMs bundled POs, so you must extract the documents as separate files and send them individually to parsing.
    • Also note that the ESD will always be the 3 business days.
  3. Mark-Up: Use the same mark up format as the RFI orders.
  4. Carrier: Use the spreadsheet "Pricing & Master Roll Info" to determine UPS vs freight, calculate price & apply 15% discount if needed.
    • If the total weight of all rolls on one order is less than 500 lbs and/or one roll is less than 150 lbs, go with UPS and write UPS and the dimensions (weight and diameter) at the bottom of the PO. Otherwise write TBD and a carrier will be sent to pick it up once the warehouse fulfills the order.
    • In addition you ust write the price next to the item which you can calculate from the spreadsheet. If the price is above $150, then use the 15% discounted price; otherwise, give the actual item price.

4. Entering Other Customer Orders

  1. Find the PO: Look for emails from Greatmats, Best Carpet, etc.
  2. Save: Save the PO to correct folder.
  3. Print & Mark-Up: Follow same process as RFI/AFM.
  4. Confirmation: Some require email confirmation (use templates).

5. Necessary NetSuite Tips

  • Fix AI Errors: Use WMS role. Edits include:
    • Change pricing
    • Edit item list
    • Adjust shipping
  • Create Acknowledgments: Go to Transactions → Order Acknowledgment → Create:
    • Select customer (ARC)
    • Enter PO#
    • Select shipping location
    • Add items, quantities
    • Verify price/ship date
