Welcome to the ARC Employee Bible

This internal guide is designed to help ARC employees quickly learn and navigate the tools and workflows used daily in our operations. Whether you're onboarding or brushing up on workflows, this guide has everything you need.

Each section focuses on a key process or tool used at ARC, particularly for order entry, NetSuite management, and communication protocols. The content is kept simple, visual, and concise so you can find what you need fast.

What You'll Learn:

  • Step-by-step breakdown of handling RFI and AFM orders
  • How to mark-up POs and send to parsing
  • Tips for using NetSuite efficiently
  • Troubleshooting + Miscellaneous internal knowledge
