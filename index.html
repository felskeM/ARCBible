1. Intro to NetSuite

  • Login: Open NetSuite in your browser and log in using company credentials.
  • Roles: You’ll use two roles — OFFICE and WMS. WMS handles order creation and shipping; OFFICE can edit customers and financials.
  • Customize: Feel free to personalize your dashboard layout.

2. Entering RFI (Flooring Inc.) Orders

Begin by opening the Outlook inbox to the Customer Service account (customerservice@athleticrc.com) and double clicking the PO PDF so that it's open in Adobe.

  1. Right-click on the PO attachment and save to the "RFI Purchase Orders" folder.
  2. Right-click again on the PO attachment and print the attachent to any non-label printer. You can now delete the email.
  3. Mark-up the order for the warehouse and send the file to parsing (arcorderparsin@pliteq.com)
  4. When marking-up, put on the top right: Shipping state, material and thickness, shipping due date, and the N.S. S-number.

3. Entering AFM (American Floor Mats) Orders

  • Open the PO: Open the email from the inbox and view the PDF in Adobe.
  • Extract: Click the edit tab and highlight all the pages of the PO.
  • Save: Right-click and press extract all files. Click on the AFM Purchase Orders folder for the extract destination.
  • Print: Print the PO to a non-label printer and delete the email if needed.
  • Mark-Up: Add the shipping state, material/thickness, ship date, and N.S. number to the top right of the printed PO.
  • Send: Email the PO file to arcorderparsin@pliteq.com with this format "Ship {customer expected ship date} from "{Either WI or AZ}".
  • Carrier Choice: Use our Pricing Master Roll Info excel sheet to determine whether or not the PO will need to be palleted or ship through UPS. Go to the dim calc page to determine the weights and dimensions. If each roll weighs less than 150 lbs and the total weight with all rolls is less than $500, then you can ship through UPS and write UPS on the bottom of the page. Otherwise, just write TBD on the bottom of the page. Write down the correct price with the same spreadsheet on the AFM Price Calc tab. All orders $150 or higher are also discounted 15% total.
  • NetSuite Check: Let's navigate to NetSuite and type in the PO number in the search bar. Write down our S reference number under the date you wrote on the PO.

4. Entering Other Customer Orders

  • Find the PO: Look in the inbox for POs from Greatmats, Best Carpet, Kiefer, or Kodiak Sports.
  • Save: Save the PO to the correct folder based on the customer.
  • Print & Mark-Up: Print the order and mark it up like RFI/AFM.
  • Confirmation: Some customers need an order confirmation. Use templates found in their folders.

5. Necessary NetSuite Tips

  • Fix AI Errors: Use the WMS role to edit orders. This is a daily task.
    Common Edits:
    • Change pricing
    • Edit item list
    • Adjust shipping location
  • Create Acknowledgments: Can be done in WMS or OFFICE role. Go to: Transactions → Order Acknowledgment → Create.
    Steps:
    • Choose customer with (ARC)
    • Enter PO#
    • Select shipping location
    • Add correct items and quantities
    • Verify price and ship date
