3. Entering AFM (American Floor Mats) Orders

  1. Open and extract the PO: Print the PO and view the PDF in Adobe and go to the edit tab. Extract the PDF as separate files to the AFM Orders folder.
  2. Send: Email the individually extracted files to arcorderparsing@pliteq.com with the same subject and contents as the RFI format.
    • DocParser cannot create an SO from AFMs bundled POs, so you must extract the documents as separate files and send them individually to parsing.
    • Also note that the ESD will always be the 3 business days.
  3. Mark-Up: Use the same mark up format as the RFI orders.
  4. Carrier: Use the spreadsheet "Pricing & Master Roll Info" to determine UPS vs freight, calculate price & apply 15% discount if needed.
    • If the total weight of all rolls on one order is less than 500 lbs and/or one roll is less than 150 lbs, go with UPS and write UPS and the dimensions (weight and diameter) at the bottom of the PO. Otherwise write TBD and a carrier will be sent to pick it up once the warehouse fulfills the order.
    • In addition you ust write the price next to the item which you can calculate from the spreadsheet. If the price is above $150, then use the 15% discounted price; otherwise, give the actual item price.
