2. Entering RFI (Flooring Inc.) Orders

  1. From your inbox, save the POs to the RFI Purchase Orders folder. Then print the POs.
  2. Mark-up the printed document and send the file to parsing (arcorderparsin@pliteq.com). The contents of the email should say "Ship {ESD} from {WI or AZ}" and nothing else as we use the DocParser AI to create SOs in NetSuite from the POs.
    • If using GMail, you'll have to edit the subject to "Emailing: Purchase Order_{PO#}"
    • If you have setup an IMAP configuration from Gmail to Outlook, just go to file explorer and right-click to send file as an attachment.
  3. Mark-up info: In the top right of the doucment, write in order (from top to bottom): thickness and color first, ESD below, finally the sales order number last.
    • The ESD can be determined as follows: Next-Day = next business day, Quick Ship = next 3 business days, no comment = next 5 business days.

  4. RFI gives us their preferred shipping method--Usually being FedEx (if within dimension limits), R&L, SAIA, or ABF.
    • For FedEx orders, you will need to login to our FedEx account and create a new shipment.
    • For all carriers, we have a BOL template that you can overwrite and create 2 hard copies of. You will also need to put the address and SO# on our skid tag template and print out one. The warehouse will let you know if they ever need more skid tags.
